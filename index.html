The Property

Your Private Rainforest Haven

Escape to tranquility in this charming vacation home nestled amidst the lush beauty of El Yunque Rainforest in serene Río Grande, Puerto Rico.

Entire Home · 8 Guests · 3 King Beds

Tucked into the hillside beside El Yunque National Forest, Casa Golden Coqui offers a genuine rainforest escape without sacrificing modern comfort. Named for Puerto Rico's beloved golden tree frog, this home wraps you in nature's embrace.

Step onto multiple balconies for morning coffee with breathtaking mountain vistas, then spend your days exploring nearby beaches, waterfalls, and natural pools — and return each evening to the soothing chorus of coquis echoing through the forest.

Location & Attractions

Explore the Area

Casa Golden Coqui is your basecamp for the best of northeast Puerto Rico — rainforest, beaches, swimming holes, and incredible restaurants, all within easy reach. These are Jessica & Josh's personal favorites.

🌅
Playa Piñones
25 min drive
Gorgeous sunsets and great for swimming. A well-known beach with a long breakwater that protects it from the Atlantic waves.
View on map →
🌴
Playa Azul
25 min drive · Josh & Jessica's family favorite!
Fun waves! A long stretch of sand lined with picture-perfect palm trees — a great mix of sun and shade.
View on map →
🏝️
Playa La Selva
25 min drive
A gorgeous untouched beach on a reserve — huge and often deserted. Requires a short jungle walk. Big, fun waves. Check rip current warnings before swimming.
View on map →
🍢
Luquillo Kiosks & Beach
25 min drive
~60 family-owned kiosks selling food, drinks, and souvenirs along a calm swimming beach. Wave runner rentals available too.
View on map →
🏄
Playa La Pared
25 min drive
Surfing beach with a surf school on site. At the point between here and Playa Azul, there's great coral for snorkeling.
View on map →
🌙
Mar Chiquita Beach
1 hr 15 min drive
One of the most popular natural pools on the north coast. A half-moon cove formed by two limestone outcroppings. Great for swimming, snorkeling, and tide pools.
View on map →
🐢
Jobo's Beach
2 hr 15 min drive
Scenic beach and popular surf site with clear waters and rock formations. Protected area where you can often spot sea turtles swimming around you. Good snorkeling near the rocks.
View on map →
🤿
Peña Blanca Beach
2 hr 30 min drive · $5 cash parking
Wonderful beach for snorkeling — bring your own gear or rent nearby. Well worth the drive for serious snorkelers.
View on map →
⭐
Playa Flamenco — Culebra
50 min to ferry + 55 min ferry · $5/person
Rated one of the top 3 beaches in the world. Great snorkeling and swimming. Cash only entry. Ferry from Ceiba — purchase tickets in advance.
Beach map →   Ferry tickets →
🐠
Playa Malones — Culebra
50 min to ferry + 55 min ferry
Amazing snorkeling and sea treasure hunting with tons of sea fans, coral, and diverse fish. No entry fee but bring your own snorkel gear. Same ferry as Flamenco.
Beach map →   Ferry tickets →
💧
Los Pilones Waterfall
13 min drive
Beautiful waterfalls and a large swimming hole. Hike is a little difficult in spots, ~20–25 min. Park on the road by the fence. Worth every step!
View on map →
🌊
Cascada Río Espíritu Santo
15 min drive
Hidden waterfall and swimming hole just steps from the road, with a panoramic ocean overlook high in a lesser-known corner of El Yunque.
View on map →
🌳
El Yunque National Forest
20 min drive · $8 entry fee
The only tropical rainforest in the US national forest system. Nearly 29,000 acres of hiking trails, waterfalls, and breathtaking mountain rivers.
View on map →
🛝
Las Paylas Natural Water Slide
30 min drive · $5–10/car cash only
Smooth river rocks form natural water slides and swimming holes. On private property — small entry fee, cash only. An absolute blast!
View on map →
✨
Bioluminescent Bay — Fajardo
45 min drive
Kayak under the stars as the bay glows blue-green when the water is disturbed. Best during a new moon. Many tour companies — call ahead to reserve.
View on map →
🏙️
Old San Juan
35 min drive
16th-century historic site with colorful houses, cobbled streets, shady plazas, great cocktail bars, and renowned Caribbean fusion restaurants.
View on map →
🏞️
Charco Azul Slot Canyon
1 hr 15 min drive + 15 min walk
A slot canyon in the jungle with a spring-fed stream — swim and jump off rocks into cool fresh water. Pair with Mar Chiquita Beach for a perfect day trip. ⚠️ Do NOT visit on rainy days.
View on map →

⚠️ Always check rip current warnings before ocean swimming: weather.gov/sju/beach_usvi
